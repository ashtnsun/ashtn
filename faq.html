Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the most common questions we get. If you don’t find the answer you're looking for, don’t hesitate to contact us!

How do I join?

Contact us to join our email list or come to one of our Callouts at the beginning of each Semester. You will then get an email listing out more information about the team. All you will have to do after this is pay dues to officially join!

How much swimming experience do I need to join the team?

We welcome swimmers of all levels of experience. Our coaches will usually have different practice intervals to tailor the workout intensity to your level of fitness. We do not provide swim lessons, so at a minimum, you will need to know how to swim.

How much are dues and what do they cover?

Dues are about $95 per semester. These cover meet, hotel, and travel fees throughout the semester as well as complementary apparel.

When and where is practice?

Practices are held at the Morgan J. Burke Aquatic Center on Monday-Friday. Practices run from 6:30-7:30 PM Monday, Wednesday, and Friday and are from 8:30-9:30 PM on Tuesday and Thursday. All practices are optional.

What are practices like?

Coaches will typically write sets in the 2800-3000 yard range per practice with intervals occasionally varying by lanes. Each day will have a different focus (ex: IM, stroke, sprint, distance, etc.) and are all optional to the individual. Our sets focus on quality rather than quantity, though still aim to challenge you. Our student coaches simply want you to have fun and help you achieve your goals!

What gear do I need for practice?

All you will need is a swimsuit, cap, and goggles. Kickboards and pull buoys are provided at the pool.

How many swim meets are there per semester?

We typically have about 3-4 swim meets per semester and usually have at least 1 away meet per semester. All of these meets are optional.

How far away are swim meets?

Swim meets can be anywhere from 1-2 hours (e.g., Northwestern) to 4-5 hours away (e.g., MSU or Ohio State). For all meets that are further away, we will stay overnight in a hotel.

What does our TYR partnership entail?

This TYR discount allows you to get a variety of discounts listed on our Team Store:

  • 25% off all products decorated or in line for team members
  • 50% off all technical suits for CCS National Qualifiers
  • Free goggles of choice for CCS National Qualifiers
